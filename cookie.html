Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform, how we use them, and your options regarding their use. By using Devspare, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide website owners with information about users’ browsing behavior.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of the platform, including login and navigation.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the platform by tracking actions like page visits and error messages.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable us to remember your preferences and settings, such as language or theme.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to your interests based on your browsing history.

3. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have several options regarding the use of cookies:

  • You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our platform.
  • Most browsers allow you to block cookies or notify you when a cookie is being placed on your device. For more details, please refer to your browser’s help section.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device. These third parties include analytics services, advertising partners, and social media platforms. These cookies are used to analyze trends, serve targeted ads, and improve services.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post any updates on this page with the updated date.

6. Contact Us
